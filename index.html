The Project Implicit is a NPO (non profit organisation) that allows for international collaboration between researcher who wish to explore implicit social cognition, or mor simply: "thoughts and feelings outside of conscious awareness and control". It allows for the edication of the public in terms of hidden biases and as a secondary objective, provides a virtual lab of data collection in the digital space.
