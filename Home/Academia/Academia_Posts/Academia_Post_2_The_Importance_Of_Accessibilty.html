#2, The Importance of Accessibility

Accessibility refers to the ease of use of something. The ability the item has to be used by everyone. This may be for pure convinience but it often does - and I believe it always should - take into consideration those with disabilities. Over the years,, several features have been implemented to make it easier for people to overcome the barriers that their diabilities create.

An example of this is ARIA. Also known as Accessible Rich Internet Applications.

Initially ARIA worked in conjunction with HTML4 - through supplementation, the interactions available were passed along to assistive technologies. However in the HTML5 update, there was an intergration of ARIA into the HTML that allowed it to be more comfortably used by developers for all audiences. Alongside this, the use of semantic HTML elements also played a great role.

Often, people who use assistive tech, find it hard to upgrade. They are reluctant because they are afraid of losing the their ability to interact on the internet and with their computers. Good Semantic Markup is something that makes this switch easier for most as it uses the keyboards built in functions to allow interactivity. The code itself is also more legible and therefore understandable.

Another important factor is just the general use of language you choose. With disabilities, screen readers are some of the most popular ARIA and can easily be stumped by certain written conventions. For example, if one was to list numbers 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 - they would sinply type it as 1 - 7, however, screen readers will not see it as one to seven but as one, dash, seven. Simply typing 1 to 7 would easily be a considerate move for those who use screen readers as it will be read as such accordingly.

The use of semantic markup may be better in terms of accesibility for the disabled by use of keyboard funtions, but it also makes it easier for people to read and uderstand what you have done and coded. Another functional role of semantic mark up in terms of accessiblility is that it easier for your work to be found by search engines.
