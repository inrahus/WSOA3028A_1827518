2020 so far.

Looking back, 2020 has been so wild already. It feels like it's been almost three years in one. I got into a relationship, and I watched my best frined fall in love too. I saw my friends thrive as artists and I saw the world fall apart with a pandemic and I was shocked when my own country was able to hold it together.

More than anything, especially after last year, I was so surprised that I got a chance to be this happy.

So I've decided to share some of my favourite memories of the year so far... And tell you a bit about them!!

This was my first date. With my boyfriend. We met last year during exams, but we really got to know each other earlier this year... We went to Rocomamas! He had a cocktail and I had a beer, and after that we went to watch a movie together. We've been dating for four months now and we get along really well...

He's one of the best things that's ever happened to me... And he makes me so happy :)

I think this is one o the most beautiful images I've ever taken - and I think it speaks to my fined's beauty as well honestly... It was a great night overall - we all got back to res safe, but, we were wasTED. :D
