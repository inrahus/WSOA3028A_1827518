corona and the government.

Hey peoplewho still actually read this site's posts it'sbeen quite a while.

I'd like to blame it on the wine, anxiety and general lack of motivation.

But today, I come bringing great news (that you would have already heard).

About a week ago, a few people came back from Italy and tested positive for COVID-19 - but luckily, there was already the testing implemented at out airports that occured immediately after landing and they were caught and quarantined on site. There's been no infections in our country so far except for that incident, but there's no international travel allowed. I mean, it's better than what's happening all around Europe lately and not to mention the Americas.

But it's nice to know!

It doesn't seem like university will be closing, I'll be staying at res and life here in Johannesburg, South Africa seems like it's going to continue as normal for now. But you never really know what could happen...

But as it stands - I'd like to thank the government (especially Cyril-Mama (Mama = Uncle)), our amazing president for protecting us from the shit storm pandemic we had coming our way.
