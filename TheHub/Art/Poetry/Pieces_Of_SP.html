you make me happy when skies are grey.     never have i ever.     this time.     6.

you make me happy when skies are grey.

it's alright when there's sunshine.

it's alright.

and when there isn't and
the storms rage on and the winds blow me from where i stand and
the rain insists on blinding my vision,

i wander.

i wander, wonder,
wait.

the wind that is blowing
me back, away
from you -
is it worth fighting?

the rain,
blinding me, is it just me
crying?

I wonder if maybe you're better off without me on these days.

I know you're better.

I know you deserve better.

maybe, maybe
i should know better.

but it's alright
when there's sunshine.

this time.

i didn't fight
you.

i didn't hate
it
or you.

and it didn't
hurt.

but you told me
it was normal
and you said that -
this time,
you'd do it again because
it was love.

6.

we were 6,
but you knew.

we were 6, but you knew.

you knew.

you knew.
you knew.
you knew.
you knew.

you.

you knew it was
wrong.

you knew,
and you used
what you
knew
to tell me what
love is.
