you make me happy when skies are grey.            

you make me happy when skies are grey.

it's alright when there's sunshine.

it's alright.

and when there isn't and
the storms rage on and the winds blow me from where i stand and
the rain insists on blinding my vision,

i wander.

i wander, wonder,
wait.

the wind that is blowing
me back, away
from you -
is it worth fighting?

the rain,
blinding me, is it just me
crying?

I wonder if maybe you're better off without me on these days.

I know you're better.

I know you deserve better.

maybe, maybe
i should know better.

but it's alright
when there's sunshine.
